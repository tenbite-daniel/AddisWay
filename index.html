Our Story

AddisWay started with me, Tenbite Daniel, and a few university friends who faced daily struggles commuting in Addis Ababa.

Taxis often overcharged or followed confusing routes, and finding the right stop took too much time. We needed a better solution.

So we built AddisWay â€” a simple app to show the best taxi routes, nearby stops, and fair prices. Making travel in the city easier, safer, and smarter for everyone.

To empower people in Addis Ababa to travel easily, safely, and fairly by providing clear taxi routes and fare information.

We want a city where everyone can move without stress, know the best way to get anywhere, and pay the right price for transport. AddisWay will make public transport simple and fair for all.
